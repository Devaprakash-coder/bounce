GAMES GURUS

Like most things in life, Ping Pong is more fun with a group of your pals. At Bounce you don’t need mad Ping Pong skills to have an epic time.

Get ready to experience Wonderball, a high tech, immersive experience where you choose what to play from our selection of games.

The rules for the table are easy, throw the ball and eliminate all your competitors cups and walk away a champion, it’s as simple as that. Beer pong, prosecco pong or whatever your poison is, if you sink ’em you drink ’em!

What’s shuffleboard we hear you ask? Well it’s kind of like bowling meets air hockey meets curling. But also kind of not. All you need to know is it’s great craic with laughs guaranteed!

Sit back, strap in and get ready to meet our awesome team of Games Gurus.
